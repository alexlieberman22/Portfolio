CSU Knitting and Crochet Club

Welcome to the CSU Knit and Crochet Club!! Our website is where you will get updates about meetings, themes for projects, wise words from our one and only president and much more! This club is a place for you to relax and work on things you would like to work on. Remember that no experience is necessary and be sure to invite all your friends. We are happy to expand our Ramily and hope to see it continue to grow through this semester, year, and hopefully many years to come.
